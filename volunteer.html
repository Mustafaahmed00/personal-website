JP Morgan - Code for Good Hackathon Event 2023

  • In the initial selection round of Code for Good 2023, I faced two coding challenges on the HackerRank platform, designed to evaluate my proficiency in handling strings, arrays, basic data structures, and collections.
  • progressed to the second phase of the selection process: the Virtual Interview. Conducted through the HireVue platform, the interview format entailed responding to two behavioral questions via recorded videos, with a time limit of 2 minutes for each question.

  • Making it to the third round meant diving into a 24-hour hackathon. In this stage, participants were tasked with developing a solution tailored for a nonprofit organization, with the flexibility to choose between frontend or backend web technologies based on personal preference.
  • I selected HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as my tools for frontend development, with the goal of crafting a tailored solution that meets the specific needs of the nonprofit organization within the 24-hour hackathon timeframe.

Be MY Eyes

  • I volunteered with Be My Eyes, an organization that connects blind and low-vision people with sighted volunteers through a mobile app for real-time visual assistance.
  • As a volunteer, I provided descriptions and guidance via video calls, helping users with tasks and navigation, thus fostering greater independence.
  • This experience deepened my empathy and understanding of the visually impaired community's challenges, reinforcing the value of technology in creating a more inclusive society.

Khan Academy

  • I am proud to have dedicated my time and skills as a Volunteer Contributor at Khan Academy, a renowned nonprofit organization committed to providing free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere.
  • My work focused on developing accessible learning materials across a variety of complex topics, including Differential Equations, Calculus, General Relativity, Gravity, and Quantum Mechanics. By breaking down these advanced concepts into understandable segments, I aimed to simplify subjects often perceived as challenging and to inspire a passion for learning in students around the world.
